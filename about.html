Why Choose Us

The Best Trusted Farms For You

Founded in 2006 AD, Om Sai Baba (OSB) Suppliers was established by Mr. Tanka Prasad Gautam(Kumar), a visionary with over 34 years of experience in the produce industry. With a deep commitment to quality and sustainability, Mr. Tanka Prasad Gautam(Kumar) set out to revolutionize the way fresh produce is supplied to hotels and restaurants across Nepal. Drawing on decades of expertise and a passion for excellence, OSB Suppliers has grown into a trusted leader in delivering premium fruits, vegetables, and dairy products, reflecting the founder’s dedication to superior service and quality.

  • Values

    We value excellence in delivering premium, fresh produce and strive to build a strong, quality-focused brand. We are committed to supporting local farmers and offering a diverse range of global products. Customer satisfaction and health are central to our values, ensuring top quality and freshness in every product.

  • Vision

    Our vision is to be the premier provider of top-tier fruits, vegetables,herbs and dairy products, transforming the supply chain for hotels, restaurants, and organizations across Nepal. We aim to elevate culinary standards while driving sustainability and fostering local partnerships. By championing eco-conscious practices and delivering exceptional quality, we aspire to enhance the reputation of our clients and become the catalyst for a more efficient and responsible supply ecosystem.

  • Mission

    At Om Sai Baba (OSB) Suppliers, our mission is to provide premium-quality fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and herbs to hotels, restaurants, and organizations, enhancing their culinary experiences with exceptional freshness. We strive to be a trusted partner by simplifying procurement, supporting local agriculture, and bridging local and global markets to drive success and excellence in the food industry.

Our Goals

Om Sai Baba (OSB) Suppliers is your trusted partner for delivering top-tier fruits, vegetables, herbs and dairy products across Nepal. We source the finest produce both locally and internationally to provide hotels and restaurants with unmatched freshness and variety. Our reliable logistics ensure prompt delivery, while our customer-first approach guarantees that your specific needs are met with precision. With a focus on quality and sustainability, OSB Suppliers elevates the standards of produce supply for the hospitality industry.

Short Term: Expand operations across Nepal to supply fresh produce to hotels and restaurants, while collaborating with local farmers to create job opportunities.

Long Term: Position Om Sai Baba (OSB) Suppliers as the leading provider of premium harvests in Nepal, known for quality and reliability, and set new industry standards by promoting sustainable growth and enriching local communities.
